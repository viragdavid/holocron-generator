Title: Parliock system/Legends
Main: The Parliock system was located in the Ariarch sector , a part of the New Territories portion of the Mid Rim . It shared its name with the Peacekeeping of Parliock , a conflict in which members of the Jedi Order were involved at some point prior to the Clone Wars of 22 BBY to 19 BBY .
Behind the scenes: The Parliock system was established in the StarWars.com Online Companion to the 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas , which placed the system in grid square J-6.
