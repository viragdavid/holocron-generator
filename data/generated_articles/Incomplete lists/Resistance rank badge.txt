Title: Resistance rank badge
Main: Resistance rank badges were insignia of military rank used by officers of the Resistance . They consisted in a gray rectangular badge with markings. Said markings were red for army personnel, and blue for navy . Although she was a Resistance general , Leia Organa wore a rank-free vest. In addition to rank badges, Resistance command officers wore brown-colored uniforms with repurposed Rebel Alliance starbirds on their left shoulder, while the medical services wore a darker variant with a medical services armband. Ground Logistics Division controllers wore large, faded-yellow utilitarian working jackets while junior controllers and lead dispatchers wore variations on the brown-colored officer's uniform.
