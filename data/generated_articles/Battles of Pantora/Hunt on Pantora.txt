Title: Hunt on Pantora
Main: Between 19 BBY and 9 BBY , the Jedi-hunting Inquisitor known as the Third Sister carried out a hunt for Jedi or Force-sensitives on the Outer Rim moon Pantora . The hunt was part of the Galactic Empire 's Inquisitorius ' task to cull survivors of the Jedi Purge.
Behind the scenes: The hunt on Pantora was mentioned in the 2023 reference book Star Wars: Dawn of Rebellion: The Visual Guide , written by Pablo Hidalgo and Emily Shkoukani .
